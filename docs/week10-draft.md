**Project:** Linear Probes for Compassion Measurement

---

## Slide 1: What problem does this solve?

**We train LLMs to be compassionate, but we can't see inside them.**

Current evaluation (Animal Harm Benchmark) scores outputs — what the model says. But a model could produce compassionate text without internal alignment.

**The gap:** No way to measure whether compassion training actually changes how the model represents problems internally.

---

## Slide 2: Your approach

**Linear probes on model activations**

- Generate contrastive pairs: same question, compassionate vs non-compassionate response
- Extract hidden states from target model (Llama 3.1 8B)
- Train classifier to distinguish them
- The learned direction = "compassion" in activation space

[Placeholder visual: diagram showing Question → Model → Activations → Probe → Score]

---

## Slide 3: Current progress/evidence

**It works better than expected**

- 105 contrastive pairs from AHB questions
- 95.2% accuracy, 0.995 AUROC at layer 8
- Tested 6 layers: performance decreases with depth

**Unexpected finding:** Optimal layer is 8 (25% depth), not ~75% as steering literature suggests. Compassion encoded early.

[Visual: layer comparison chart or just the results table]

## Slide 4: Biggest uncertainty or hurdle

**Are we measuring compassion or confounds?**

- Pairs are AHB-derived → can't independently validate AHB
- Both responses generated by Claude → stylistic artifacts?
- 1950s framing for non-compassionate → detecting era, not values?

**We can claim:** Probe detects AHB-style compassion in activations.
**We cannot claim:** Independent measurement of "true" compassion.

---

## Slide 5: What feedback do you need most?

1. **Negative controls:** What non-compassion text should we test to prove specificity? (Technical docs? Math? Weather?)

2. **Generalization:** What compassion scenarios outside AHB would be convincing? (Human ethics? Environmental?)

3. **The layer finding:** Is early encoding (layer 8) surprising? Does this change how CaML should apply interventions?

---

## What's testable now

- The probe exists and runs
- Can score any text for "compassion" on 0-1 scale
- Can compare activations across models

## What remains

- Generalization testing (non-AHB scenarios)
