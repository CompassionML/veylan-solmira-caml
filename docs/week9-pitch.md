# Week 9: Refining Your Pitch

**Project:** Linear Probes for Compassion Measurement

---

## The Pitch (3-6 sentences)

**Problem:** We can train AI systems to produce compassionate responses about animals, but current evaluation only measures what models say — not whether they've internalized compassionate values. A model could learn to give the "right" answers without genuine alignment, and we'd have no way to detect this gap.

**Solution:** I'm building a tool that measures compassion directly inside AI models by analyzing their internal representations. Using linear probes on model activations, we can detect whether a model encodes compassion when processing a question — not just whether it outputs compassionate text. Early results show 95% accuracy distinguishing compassionate from non-compassionate reasoning at the model's internal level.

**Career connection:** This project develops skills in mechanistic interpretability — understanding AI systems from the inside — which is central to AI safety work. I'm building toward a career ensuring AI systems are genuinely aligned with values like compassion, not just superficially compliant.
